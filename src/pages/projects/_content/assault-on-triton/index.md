### How to Play

- **W** / **Up Arrow** – Move forward
- **A** / **Left Arrow** – Strafe Left
- **S** / **Down Arrow** – Move Backward
- **D** / **Right Arrow** – Strafe Right
- **Space** – Shoot Blaster
- **E** - Fire E-Bomb

Destroy all enemies and avoid getting hit. Pickup powerups to get stronger and survive longer.
